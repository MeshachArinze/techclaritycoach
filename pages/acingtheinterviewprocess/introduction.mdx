The interview process is a critical stage in your journey towards transitioning into a tech career. It is an opportunity
for potential employers to assess your skills, experience, and cultural fit, while also allowing you to evaluate the
company and its offerings. To successfully navigate this stage and secure a job offer, it is essential to be well-prepared
and confident. In this section, we will provide guidance and tips for acing the interview process, covering various aspects
such as technical interviews, behavioral questions, and negotiation strategies. By the end of this section, you will have
the tools and knowledge to effectively showcase your skills, demonstrate your passion for technology, and make a lasting
impression on your potential employers.
