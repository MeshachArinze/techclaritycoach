Creating a strong portfolio is a crucial aspect of showcasing your skills, experience, and passion for technology as you
transition into a tech career. A well-curated portfolio can help you stand out in the competitive job market and demonstrate
your ability to excel in your desired role. In this section, we will guide you through the process of building an impressive
portfolio that highlights your accomplishments, projects, and expertise. We will cover topics such as personal branding,
developing and showcasing your skills through projects, contributing to open source projects, and pursuing freelancing
opportunities or side projects. Additionally, we will discuss the value of participating in hackathons, competitions, and
other events to further enhance your portfolio. By diligently building and maintaining your portfolio, you can increase your
chances of securing your ideal tech job and successfully transitioning into your new career.
